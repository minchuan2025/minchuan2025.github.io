IMPROVING FLOW MATCHING BASED TEXT-TO-SPEECH WITH PREFERENCE-ALIGNED OPTIMIZATION AND CLASSIFIER-FREE GUIDANCE

Minchuan Chen, Chenchen Wan, Peng Qi, Shaojun Wang, Jing Xiao

Ping An Technology, China
Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China

Abstract

Flow matching is a powerful generative model and has become a core component of modern text-to-speech (TTS) systems. In order to ensure high-quality speech synthesis, Classifier-Free Guidance (CFG) is widely used during the training and inference stages of flow matching based TTS models. However, in the scenario of zero-shot voice cloning, the models keep human feedback isolated from training and inference, which results in mismatched training objectives and evaluation metrics. In this work, we propose a preference optimization method by separately modeling preferred and dispreferred information with two distinct models, which enables better utilization of preferred and dispreferred data. We further incorporate an improved classifier-free guidance technique to enhance synthesis adherence to the text and reference audio. The experiments demonstrate that these optimizations significantly enhance intelligibility, speaker similarity and naturalness of synthesized speech. We also verify that the proposed model outperforms the baseline TTS model, requiring only a small amount of preference data for training.

Audio Samples

For the pretraining phase, we use WenetSpeech4TTS Premium, a Mandarin dataset contains 945 hours of multi-speaker corpus, and LibriTTS dataset, a multi-speaker English corpus comprising approximately 585 hours of speech, as the training sets. For DPO, we construct different preference speech datasets (Data1, Data2 and Data3) for training.

Baseline: Pretrained F5-TTS Small model.
PA-Base-DT1 : Single model DPO training with Data1 (preference pairs constructing by ground truth samples and generated samples of the pretrained model).
PA-Base-DT2 : Single model DPO training with Data2 (preference pairs constructing by ranking regular text-audio pairs with WER and SSIM metrics).
PA-Dual-DT1 : Proposed dual model DPO training with Data1 (preference pairs counstructing by ground truth samples and speech samples generated by the pretrained model).
PA-Dual-DT2 : Proposed dual model DPO training with Data2 (preference pairs constructing by regular text-audio pairs with WER and SSIM metrics).
PA-Dual-DT3 : Proposed dual model DPO training with Data3 (preference pairs constructing by challenging text-audio pairs with WER and SSIM metrics).
